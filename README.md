# AtliQ-Grands
AtliQ Grands is a fictional hotel chain operating across major cities in India- Delhi, Mumbai, Bangalore, and Hyderabad for over 20 years. 

The company offers multiple hotel categories such as:
Luxury: Atlix Palace
Business: Atlix Seasons
Others: Exotica, Atlix Bay

They also provide different room types:
Standard
Elite
Premium
Presidential

Bookings are made through:
Atlix’s official website
Third-party platforms (e.g., MakeMyTrip, etc.)

##Business Challenge
Atlix Grands is facing:
- Declining revenue
- Loss of market share
- Increased competition




To address this, management has decided to adopt data-driven decision making.
